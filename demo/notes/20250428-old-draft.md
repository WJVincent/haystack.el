## The argument

Plain text files are the most durable digital format. Every operating
system can read them. Every editor can open them. They'll be readable
in 50 years.

Proprietary formats die. Remember OneNote's .one files? Evernote's
.enex? Even Notion's export is lossy.

## The counterargument

Plain text lacks structure. You can't embed images inline (well, org
can sort of do this). You can't have rich formatting without a render
step. Collaboration is harder.
