Sourdough Bread — Basic Recipe

Starter: 100g active, fed 8-12 hours ago, passes float test

Ingredients:
  500g bread flour
  350g water (70% hydration)
  100g starter
  10g salt

Timeline:
  9am  — mix flour + water, autolyse 30 min
  9:30 — add starter, squish to combine
  10:00 — add salt + 25g reserved water, slap & fold
  10:30 to 2:30 — bulk ferment, stretch & fold every 30 min (4-6 sets)
  2:30 — pre-shape, bench rest 20 min
  3:00 — final shape, into banneton
  3:15 — fridge overnight (12-16 hours cold retard)

Next morning:
  preheat Dutch oven at 500F for 45 min
  score the dough
  bake covered 20 min at 500F
  uncover, reduce to 450F, bake 20-25 min more
  cool on wire rack at least 1 hour before cutting

Notes:
  - hydration can go up to 80% once you're comfortable
  - the window pane test is more reliable than timing
  - under-fermented is worse than over-fermented
  - ear formation depends on scoring angle (30 degrees)
